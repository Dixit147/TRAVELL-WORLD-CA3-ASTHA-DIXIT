New YORK CITY

NEW York City is a global leader in many industries, including technology, finance, fashion, and health care. It's also known for its cultural institutions, parks, and restaurants. Here are some other things to know about New York City:

Population

New York City is the most populated city in the United States, with a population density of over 27,000 people per square mile.

Diversity

New York City is known as the "melting pot of the world" because of its ethnic and linguistic diversity. People from all over the world speak more than 200 languages in the city.

Economy

New York City is the world's most economically powerful city, with the world's two largest stock exchanges. The New York metropolitan area has the largest metropolitan economy in the world.

Culture

New York City is considered the cultural capital of the world, and many American cultural movements originated there.

Boroughs

New York City is made up of five boroughs: Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, the Bronx, and Staten Island.
